Mango Pickle

Our signature mango pickle is prepared with raw, hand-picked mangoes, blended with aromatic spices and cold-pressed oils. The tangy and spicy taste is a perfect companion for every meal.

Lemon Pickle

Sun-cured lemons mixed with traditional spice blends create a zesty and piquant flavor. Our lemon pickle is loved for its balance of tang, salt, and spice, making it an authentic Telugu delicacy.
